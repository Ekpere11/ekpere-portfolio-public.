// 04: FEATURED BUILD

Real-Time Operations & Revenue Tracking Dashboard

Built while embedded with a sales company, this system ties individual task activity directly to revenue, giving leadership one live view of who's driving results, who's falling behind, and where the business is exposed.

EXCEL · SALES OPERATIONS
● RUNNING WEEKLY
PROBLEM
Leadership couldn't measure anyone's real impact. People said they were working, but nothing tied that effort to revenue, leaving leadership flying blind on who was actually driving results and who was falling behind.
SOLUTION
Built an operations and revenue tracking system in Excel that logged each employee's tasks, completion status, turnaround time, and the revenue their work generated, then layered dashboards on top so leadership could read it at a glance. Run weekly.
RESULT (QUANTIFIED)
›38 completed tasks tracked in one week, with an average turnaround of just under two days.
›Surfaced that one employee generated $9,200 of $10,000 total revenue (~92%), enabling recognition, a promotion, and targeted coaching for the rest of the team.
›Flagged that same 92% concentration as a business risk to leadership and recommended spreading capability across the team.
›Surfaced a pending 14% of tasks needing attention, an early warning on stalled work before it became a bigger problem.
